Welcome to Apartment Security Mangement System

The purpose of this application is to help the Admins, Guards and Flat owners to manage the record and information of the visitors, vehicles, deliveries, security alerts and domestic help. With this application it will be easy for tracking all the activities performed in apartment virtually. Admin is responsible for registering Guards and Flat Owners. Guard will be able to manage visitor's in/out time records, and can also add, view package deliveries for all the flats. This system also provides a user friendly application system and a security control to ensure the data integrity.



Security Services

Flat Details

Login credentials will be provided to respective Flat Owners, which will help owners to login at ease and can view all flat related details from any where in the world...



Security Alerts

We are providing 24/7 Emergency Sevices and security alerts.In case of any emergency ,immediate actions will be taken and information will be passed to all the residents immediately.



Guard And Domestic-help Attendance

Guards and Domestic Helps attendance can be easily tracked and managed





Delivery Service

Deliveries of all your packages will be taken care by guards and the status of the same can be viewed by clicking view delivery section.



Vehicle Tracking

Capture vehicle details like number, color easily during entry and exit for record purposes. Guard will be capturing in/out time of all vehicles in an apartment.



Visitor Tracking

Replace all your manual registers at gate with a single click Ability to stop impostors trying to enter your Apartment Complex Ability to verify contact details of visitors, Ability to stop blacklisted staff from entering Faster check-ins, gets rid of queues at security gate



Domestic-Help Service

Domestic Helps like plumber, electrician... etc will be provided to flat residents and their details are recorded for further investigations in future
